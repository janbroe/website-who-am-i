Previous job

After my studies in electronics-ict, I immediately started working at Nokia. What does Nokia Antwerp do today? The Fixed Network department takes care of R&D and development of new products in the telecom sector. When developing this new hardware and equipment, everything must also be thoroughly tested. My job as a hardware dependent software engineer is to develop software so that the hardware designers can initially test their prototypes. Then they can verify that everything works as it should. When the prototypes move towards solid product, and are mass produced, my code is then used again to test the hardware before it leaves the factory. The advantage is that I am constantly working with different teams, so communication and teamwork are key.
