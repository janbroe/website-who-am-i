Who am I?

I am Jan Broeckx, 28 years young, full of ambition and looking for a new challenge and opportunity.

From a young age I have been fascinated by everything that has to do with computers, the internet or technology. Opening up old electronics, programming robots, writing my first scripts in bash, etc. My training as an industrial engineer electronics - ICT was therefore a logical step after secondary school.

I am a cheerful and inquisitive techie who is always open to new opportunities. In my spare time I am an avid athlete and enjoy playing volleyball and running, among other things. At quieter times, you can equally find me at my desk tinkering with an electronics project.
