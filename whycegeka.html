Why Cegeka?

The company Cegeka first came to my attention through a friend. That's how I found out that it was first a small Limburg family business that has grown into a very big player in IT. After more research I could conclude that for me this is by far the most interesting company in the list of Switchfully. Just the fact that consultancy is offered really appeals to me. This way you can gain experience on different projects to learn as much as possible. Cegeka also offers various services and platforms in different industries to their customers. This ensures that you do not remain restricted in your growth and are offered sufficient challenge and variety. I think the emphasis on the close contact with customers and the personalized experience that comes with it is fantastic.
